Program Description

RG Automation operates Sofia AI, an AI-powered voice receptionist that answers inbound calls for local service businesses and sends transactional SMS messages to customers. Messages include service request confirmations, technician status updates, and job completion notifications.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your service interaction. Typically 1–3 messages per service call (confirmation, status update, completion).

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us at rcgavaldon@rgaibiz.com.

Consent

By calling a business powered by Sofia AI and providing your phone number during the call, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to your service request. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Supported Carriers

Compatible with all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Cricket, Boost, and Metro by T-Mobile. Carrier support may vary.
