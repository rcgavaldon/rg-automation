Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

RG Automation ("we," "us," or "our") operates Sofia AI, an AI-powered voice receptionist and messaging service for local businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our SMS messaging services.

Information We Collect

When a customer calls a business using Sofia AI, we may collect:

  • Name provided during the call
  • Phone number (caller ID)
  • Service request details described during the call
  • Address provided for service scheduling

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Send transactional SMS messages confirming your service request
  • Provide status updates related to your service appointment (e.g., technician on the way)
  • Send a thank-you message upon job completion

SMS Messaging

By calling a business powered by Sofia AI and providing your phone number, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to your service request. These are not marketing messages. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Information may be shared with the specific local business you contacted in order to fulfill your service request.

Data Retention

Call and message data is retained for up to 90 days to support service quality and dispute resolution, then deleted.
