SMS Messaging Opt-In

Last updated: April 2026

RG Automation ("we," "us," or "our") provides Sofia AI, an AI-powered voice receptionist and messaging service. This page describes how end customers opt in to receive SMS messages from businesses powered by Sofia AI.

How Customers Opt In

Customers provide express consent to receive SMS messages through one of the following methods:

Method 1 — Inbound Phone Call

When a customer calls a Sofia AI-powered business, the automated receptionist verbally informs the caller:

"This call may be recorded for quality and training purposes. Would you like to receive a follow-up text message with your appointment or job details? Please say yes or no."

During every call, Sofia verbally asks for explicit consent before any SMS is sent. Callers must say yes — Sofia does not accept silence as consent.

Method 2 — Web Contact Form

When a customer submits a contact or quote request form on a Sofia AI client's website, the form includes a mandatory checkbox:

"I agree to receive SMS updates about my service request from this business. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out."

Types of Messages Sent

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Technician on-the-way notifications
  • Job completion summaries
  • Follow-up quote information
  • Missed appointment notifications

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on service activity. Customers typically receive 1–4 messages per service request.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details.

How to Opt Out

Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. After opting out, no further messages will be sent except a one-time confirmation of the opt-out.

Help
